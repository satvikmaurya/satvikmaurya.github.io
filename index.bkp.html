About Me

I completed my B.Tech. in Electronics and Communications Engineering from Delhi Technological University in 2018 and I will be joining the MS-Research program in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at the University of Wisconsin-Madison in the Spring of 2021. I have previously worked an R&D Engineer at Synopsys where I worked with transactors used in Synopsys' emulation platform ZeBu. As an undergraduate and as a professional, I have worked on multiple projects utilizing HW/SW Co-design architectures along with other applications requiring conventional RTL design.
My other interests include reading, football, gaming, traveling, and music.
